Lion

known as the "King of the Jungle." Lions live in groups called prides.

Bear

A bear is a large animal with thick fur. It can be brown, black, or even white

Camel

A camel is a tall animal with long legs and a hump on its back. Camels can live in hot deserts and can go for many days without water.

Cat

A cat is a small, soft animal with whiskers and a tail. Cats are very good at catching mice.

Dog

A dog is a friendly animal and often called "man's best friend." Dogs like to play, run, and fetch balls.

Fish

A fish is an animal that lives in the water. It has scales and fins that help it swim. Fish come in many colors and shapes

Duck

A duck is a small bird that lives in water. It has webbed feet to swim and a soft, fluffy body. Ducks like to quack and can fly, but they spend a lot of time swimming.

An elephant is a very large animal with big ears and a long trunk. Elephants are gentle and kind, and they live in groups called herds.

Wolf

A wolf is a wild animal that lives in forests. Wolves are similar to dogs but are much larger. They hunt in groups called packs and communicate with howls.

Sheep

A sheep is a fluffy animal that is often white. They have wool that people use to make clothes. Sheep like to stay in groups and eat grass.

Horse

A horse is a tall animal with long legs and a flowing mane. Horses are fast runners and are used for riding and working.

Whale

A whale is the biggest animal in the ocean. It can be very large, and it swims by moving its tail.
